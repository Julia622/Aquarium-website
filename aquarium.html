Types of Aquarium

Reef Aquarium

Probably the most popular type of marine aquarium. Its purpose is to keep fish and corals in harmony, so it is essential that there is no type of fish that eats coral.

Fish Only Aquarium

As its name suggests, it is a type of marine aquarium populated only by fish. Most of these fish feed on pagurus, shrimp, corals, anemones, and other invertebrates.

Coral Aquarium

Perfect for bringing a multitude of shapes and beautiful colors to any environment, the coral aquarium does not have fish. Some may possess only one or the other so-called pest controller, a role played by tang or wrasse.
